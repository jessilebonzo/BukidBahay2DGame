Bukid Bahay

A 2D desktop farming game that showcases Philippine crops and farms, promoting sustainable farming practices. It features pixelated graphics, user-friendly interface, and tower defense gameplay.

Features

The Bukid Bahay 2D Game Features

Splash Screen

Splash Screen

Start Game

  • The splash screen showcases the tagline "A Filipino Farm House," a farming game that harmoniously intertwines the tranquility of Philippine province life with the exciting challenges of tower defense. Diverse crops and serene visuals set the stage for your adventure. Clicking "Start" leads to the home page. The design reflects a serene farm life.

    • Dialogue Scene

    Dialogue Scene

    Dialogue Scene

  • Juan learns from Lola Isang that their family protects a "mystical land" with harmful mythical creatures. To maintain balance, Lola entrusts Juan with planting a special seed to see these creatures. Juan accepts, promising to safeguard both humans and the mythical beings on the land.
  • Players, guided by Lola Isang, receive a free seed to plant on the farm before advancing to tower defense gameplay.

    • Almanac

    Almanac

    Flora and Mythical Creatures

  • The crops are rice grains, corn, sweet potatoes, cabbage, ube, eggplant, onions, sponge gourd, radish, and garlic. In the farm game and tower defense levels, the player can plant any crop depending on their currency.
  • Level 1 chills you with berbalang invaders. Level 2 throws cunning dwendes your way. Level 3 unleashes a nightmarish tikbalang. In Level 4, face the bizarre manananggal. Finally, the grand finale pits you against the Kapre, a colossal tree giant infamous for causing mind-bending confusion! The crops you cultivate will depend on the spoils you earn by defeating these legendary creatures in the tower defense levels.

    • Store

    Store

    Store

  • After conquering enemies in the tower defense levels, you'll earn currency that can be used to purchase crops for your farm. The crops you can buy depend on the spoils you collect, so strategize your battles wisely!
  • A clear message will confirm your purchase, for example, "You bought rice!" If your funds are low, an example message like "Not enough currency for sweet potatoes" will help you plan your next move.

    • Settings & Developers

    Settings & Developers

    Settings & Developers

  • For players, a QR code unlocks the game's website, while a question mark icon playfully nudges them towards a mini-game. This mini-game keeps them busy and earning in-game currency while their crops patiently grow.
  • To modify the game's volume, long-press your mouse button to adjust the background music. Below the sound, you can mute or unmute the sound effects of the game. Click the "QUIT" if the player wants to quit the game and name of developers of the 2d game.

    • Farming Game

    Farming Game

    Farming Game

  • When the farm game begins, players receive a free rice seed to plant in the farm game. Planting the seed, waiting for growth, and harvesting yields plants for the inventory utilized in the tower defense game.
  • Once harvested, your crops will be stored in your inventory, ready to be used in the tower defense levels for strategic advantage!

    • Arcade Game & Levels

    Arcade Game & Levels

    Arcade & Levels

  • The player can play the arcade-game to earn currency and use it to buy crops. Entering the farmhouse and playing dodge the crows will add to the player's currency, which can then be used to plant crops in the farm game.
  • Levels in the Bukid Bahay Farming Game progress sequentially. Initially, only level 1 is unlocked. As you successfully complete levels, subsequent levels will unlock, allowing you to progress further. The number of unlocked levels displayed will reflect your progress in the Tower Defense levels. Completing a level will display a layout numbered 1–5, indicating the level has been opened.

    • Level 1

    Level 1

    Level 1

  • In Level 1, players face berbalangs known as ghouls. There are two soils that the player can use to plant crops and kill the mythical creatures. In this level, the player must defeat the Berbalangs in order to move on to the next level.
  • The dialogue will appear once the player has successfully defeated the mythical creatures in Level 1.

    • Level 2

    Level 2

    Level 2

  • In level 2, there are three soils that the player can use to plant crops and kill the mythical creatures. In this level, the player must defeat the Dwendes in order to move on to the next level.
  • The dialogue will appear once the player has successfully defeated the mythical creatures in Level 2.

    • Level 3

    Level 3

    Level 3

  • In Level 3, players are provided with four fertile soils strategically positioned to accommodate the planting of crops essential for combating mythical creatures that the player can use to plant crops and kill the mythical creatures. In this level, the player must defeat the Tikbalangs in order to move on to the next level.
  • The dialogue will appear once the player has successfully defeated the mythical creatures in Level 3.

    • Level 4

    Level 4

    Level 4

  • In level 4, there are five soils that the player can use to plant crops and kill the mythical creatures. In this level, the player must defeat the Manananggals in order to move on to the next level.
  • The dialogue will appear once the player has successfully defeated the mythical creatures in Level 4.

    • Level 5

    Level 5

    Level 5

  • In level 5, there are seven soils that the player can use to plant crops and kill the mythical creatures. In this level, the player must defeat the Kapres in order to move on to the next level.
  • The dialogue will appear once the player has successfully defeated the mythical creatures in Level 5.
